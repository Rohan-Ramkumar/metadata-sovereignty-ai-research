# Academic Research on Accessibility

## Easy Read Research

Chinn, D., & Homeyard, C. (2020)
Easy read and accessible information for people with intellectual disabilities: A meta-narrative literature review.

Key idea:
Accessible information can support participation and autonomy for people with intellectual disabilities.

## AAC Research

Nam, S., Kim, J., & Sparks, S. (2018)

An Overview of Review Studies on Effectiveness of Major AAC Systems.

Key idea:
AAC systems support communication for people with developmental disabilities but effectiveness varies across contexts.
