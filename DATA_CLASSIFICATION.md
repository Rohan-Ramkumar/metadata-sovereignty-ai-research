# Data Classification & Public Sharing Rules

This document defines data classes and what may be included in the public repository.

## Classes

### PUBLIC
- Non-sensitive templates (field names only)
- Project descriptions at a conceptual level
- Accessibility resources and public standards
- Sanitised/fictitious example records labelled "Fictional — sanitised for public use"

### INTERNAL
- Internal governance notes, process details, staff-only guidance
- Documents that reference internal Drive/SharePoint IDs

### SENSITIVE / RESTRICTED
- Any real case records, evidence registers, interviews, incident details
- Personal Identifying Information (PII)
- Audio/video/images of real people
- Legal, forensic, or medical notes

## Quick Rules
1. **Never** publish records that contain PII or chain-of-custody information. (E.g. SSA casefile.) :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}  
2. Publish *templates only* (field names) — not filled templates.  
3. Example records must be fully sanitised and clearly labelled as fictional/test cases.  
4. Maintain a `PUBLICATION_LOG.md` recording who approved each public addition and why (consent/authorisation).
