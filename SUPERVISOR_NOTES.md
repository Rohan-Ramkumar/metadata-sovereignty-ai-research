# Supervisor Notes
Metadata Sovereignty Alliance – Student Consulting Project

This document provides guidance, checkpoints, and feedback for the student consulting team during the project.

The purpose of this file is to keep supervision transparent and documented within the repository.

---

# Project Goal

The goal of this consulting project is to explore responsible ways artificial intelligence could support systems designed to preserve:

• authorship  
• accessibility  
• ethical data governance  
• long-term traceability of records  

The project focuses on **metadata-first infrastructure**, rather than building AI systems directly.

Students should prioritise **clarity, governance, and accessibility over technical complexity.**

---

# Key Principles

Research and recommendations should support:

• preservation of authorship  
• transparent AI processes  
• accessible communication formats  
• ethical governance of records  

Avoid recommending systems that involve:

• facial recognition  
• biometric profiling  
• automated clinical or legal decisions  
• predictive risk scoring about individuals
