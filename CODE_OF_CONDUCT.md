# Code of Conduct

This repository supports collaborative research and learning.

Participants are expected to:

• communicate respectfully
• credit sources appropriately
• respect lived-experience communities referenced in the research
• prioritise ethical and responsible AI practices

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

• harassment or discrimination
• misrepresentation of sources
• misuse of sensitive information

Concerns can be directed to:

founder@edulinked.com.au
