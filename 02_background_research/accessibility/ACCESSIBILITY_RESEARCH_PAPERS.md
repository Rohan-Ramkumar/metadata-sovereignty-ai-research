# Academic Research on Accessibility Communication

This document lists peer-reviewed research relevant to Easy Read and AAC.

---

## Easy Read Research

Chinn, D., & Homeyard, C.

Easy read and accessible information for people with intellectual disabilities: A meta-narrative literature review.

Key idea:
Accessible information supports autonomy and participation for people with intellectual disabilities.

---

## Linguistic Research on Easy Read

Fajardo, I. et al.

Easy-to-read texts for students with intellectual disability.

Key idea:
Comprehension depends on vocabulary, sentence structure, and cognitive load.

---

## AAC Research

Nam, S., Kim, J., & Sparks, S.

An Overview of Review Studies on Effectiveness of Major AAC Systems.

Key idea:
AAC systems support communication for people with developmental disabilities, but effectiveness varies depending on context.
