# Easy Read Resources

Easy Read is a communication format designed to make information easier to understand for people with intellectual disability, cognitive differences, or language processing challenges.

Key characteristics:

• simple language  
• short sentences  
• visual supports such as symbols or images  
• clear layout and structure  

---

## Key Organisations

### Inclusion Europe – Easy-to-Read Guidelines

https://www.inclusion-europe.eu/easy-to-read/

International guidelines explaining how to produce Easy Read information.

---

### European Easy-to-Read Standards

https://easy-to-read.inclusion-europe.eu/european-standards/

A comprehensive standard for producing accessible information.

---

### Australian Government Style Manual – Easy Read

https://www.stylemanual.gov.au/content-types/easy-read

Guidance used across Australian government agencies for accessible communication.

## Research Topics for Students

Students researching Easy Read should examine:

• readability and comprehension  
• document structure  
• visual communication  
• how Easy Read supports advocacy and rights communication
